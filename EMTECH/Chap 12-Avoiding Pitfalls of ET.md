# 1. The 4 Common Traps for Established Firms
- **Delayed Participation:**
	- High uncertainty (rational to **watch and wait**).
	- Suitable for narrow applications not yet required by existing customer.
	- Earlier of ET often immature and not suitable for existing customer.
- **Sticking with the Familiar:** 
	- Sticking with the known is even more appealing 有吸引力的
	- Lack of in-house capability to judge new tech.
- **Reluctance 抗拒 to Fully Commit:** Companies fear **cannibalizing 自相残杀**  their own profitable products. They often find it hard to justify investments in ET using traditional ROI (Return on Investment) criteria.
- **Lack of Persistence:** Facing pressure for quick quarterly results, firms may lose patience. They might withdraw from a new tech too early after a few missed forecasts or negative financial results
